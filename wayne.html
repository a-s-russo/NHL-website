Wayne Gretzky

Wayne Douglas Gretzky is a Canadian former professional ice hockey player and former head coach. He played 20 seasons in the NHL for four teams from 1979 to 1999. Nicknamed 'the Great One', he has been called the greatest ice hockey player ever by the NHL based on surveys of hockey writers, ex-players, general managers and coaches.

Cool stats about Wayne Gretzky

Amazing fact 1

He is the only NHL player to total over 200 points in one season.

Amazing fact 2

In May 2021, one of his 1979 hockey cards sold for US$3.75 million.

Amazing fact 3

At the time of his retirement in 1999, he held 61 NHL records.
